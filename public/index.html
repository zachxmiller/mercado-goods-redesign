The Founder
It's not about perfection. It's about the process.

Gerardo Mercado is a designer, filmmaker, and creative who built Mercado Goods as a laboratory for self-expression. Experimenting across mediums — from spray paint to selvedge denim — he creates pieces that carry culture forward without compromise. Every design is a conversation between where he comes from and where he's going.
