The Founder

Gerardo Mercado

Gerardo Mercado is a designer, filmmaker, and creative who built Mercado Goods as a laboratory for self-expression. Experimenting across mediums — from spray paint to selvedge denim — he creates pieces that carry culture forward without compromise.

Every design is a conversation between where he comes from and where he's going. Growing up between two worlds, Gerardo found beauty in the collision of tradition and modernity — and that tension became the foundation of everything Mercado Goods represents.

"
It's not about perfection. It's about the process.
— Gerardo Mercado
Our Mission

Carrying Culture Forward

Mercado Goods is more than a brand — it's a bridge between generations. Every piece we create honors the hands, stories, and traditions that came before us, while pushing forward into new territory.

We believe in small-batch production, intentional design, and the kind of craftsmanship that can't be rushed. From the fabrics we source to the artisans we collaborate with, every decision is rooted in respect — for culture, for community, and for the creative process itself.

This isn't fast fashion. This is slow, deliberate work that carries weight. When you wear Mercado Goods, you carry a piece of that story with you.
